## 1. Information We Collect

This application is designed to function with minimal data collection. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect personal identifiers such as your name, phone number, or physical address.

When the application communicates with this API, technical data such as your IP address may be logged by the server. ## 2. Third-Party Services and Analytics

We value your privacy and aim to keep the application as lean as possible:

* **No Advertisements:** This application does not contain any third-party advertisements or tracking for advertising purposes.
* **No Third-Party Analytics:** We do not use dedicated third-party analytics frameworks (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics) within the app code.
* **Platform Statistics:** We may access basic, aggregated usage statistics provided by the Google Play Store (such as install counts, device types, and crash reports). This data is aggregated by Google and does not personally identify you.
* **API Usage:** We monitor API performance and usage metrics to ensure the application remains functional and efficient.

## 3. Data Retention and Security

Because we do not collect personal user data, we do not store personal information on our servers. Any technical logs generated by API usage are rotated and deleted according to standard server maintenance protocols.

Our application does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
